Our Mission
Computer Engineering Lab and Life Sciences

Our mission is to assist students in our locla community by developing their computer science kills and incorporating biology with this rapidly growing field as a means of motivating them to build leadership and teamwork skills.

We hope that our inspiration has a lasting effect and extends past the length of this course.
