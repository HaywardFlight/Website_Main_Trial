Our Impact On Our Community

100% of the money that you donate will be given to anorganization named Room to Read, whose goal is to improve education globally in continents such as Africa and Asia. Due to COVID-19, schools across the world have closed, and over 1 billion students do not have access to education. However, Room to Read is taking action during this time to close gender inequalities as well as fostering educatoin. Our goal as CELLS is to help Room to Read in reaching their goal, and you can help us reach our goal by donating now! For every 25 dollars donated, a child in an underprivileged country will have access to education for 6 months, so just imagine the impact you can have on a global scale by donating!
